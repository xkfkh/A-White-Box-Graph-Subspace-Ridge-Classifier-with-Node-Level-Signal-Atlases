# 04 L-Series Repairs of the White-box GNN Route

Goal: repair the white-box GNN route after G-series limitations.

Explored directions:
- ReduNet-style expansion and compression;
- CRATE / ISTA-style proximal coding;
- signal-noise separation;
- temperature annealing;
- graph-aware coding rate;
- multi-scale GCN;
- adaptive gate.

Main lesson: some variants worked, but they still relied on neural training and were not ideal for the final atlas-oriented method.
